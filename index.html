About Us

Discover the Charm of Stockholm with Our Expert Tour Guide. Immerse yourself in the beauty and history of Stockholm, often called the 'Venice of the North.' Our knowledgeable guide will lead you through the city's most iconic sites, perfect for history enthusiasts, culture lovers, and curious adventurers. Enjoy the convenience of a private chauffeur who will drive you around the city, ensuring a seamless and comfortable experience.

Tour Highlights:

Gamla Stan (Old Town): Wander through charming cobblestone streets, and visit the majestic Royal Palace and the historic Storkyrkan Cathedral.

Vasa Museum: Marvel at the 17th-century warship, Vasa, and learn its incredible story.

Skansen Open-Air Museum: Discover Swedish traditions and encounter native wildlife.

Djurgården Island: Explore attractions like the ABBA Museum and the serene Rosendals Trädgård.

City Hall (Stadshuset): Enjoy breathtaking architecture and panoramic views of the city. Our guide is qualified to provide in-depth tours of this iconic building.

Modern Art and Architecture: Visit the Moderna Museet and admire the city's cutting-edge buildings.

Additional Tours:

Vasa Museum: Delve deeper into the story of the Vasa ship.Spy Program: Discover Stockholm’s hidden espionage history.

Drottningholm Palace: Visit the stunning residence of the Swedish royal family.

Royal Palace: Explore the grandeur of the Royal Palace in the heart of the city.Sigtuna Landside: Experience the charm of Sweden’s oldest town.

Hammarby Sjöstad: Discover the modern eco-friendly district of Stockholm.

World’s Tallest Underground Art: Explore the stunning artwork in Stockholm’s subway system.

Private Tours: Enjoy exclusive private tours for up to 8 people. Travel comfortably with our private car service.
